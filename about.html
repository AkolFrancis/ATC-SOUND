About

Co-founded in 2002 by Jon Cohen and Rob Stone, New York City-based ATC SOUND has put out a dynamic catalogue of critically acclaimed albums and singles worldwide. ATC SOUND is the
current home to rapidly rising singer/songwriter Clairo, Detroit native Charlie Burg, multi-talented 20-year-old Zachary Knowles, energetic Platinum certified duo Matt and Kim, electronic
DJ/Producer Super Duper, and alt-electro artist Fractures. The label has released a posthumous album from Alan Vega — prolific artist and one-half of the influential proto-punk band Suicide —
and also holds the catalogues to roster alumni YOTA: Youth of the Apocalypse featuring Jeff Wootton (Gorillaz) and Jamie Reynolds (Klaxons), Saul Williams, The Bots, Yuna, Neon Indian, Ben
Lee, Birdmonster, Future Feats, and Editors. ATC SOUND artists have performed on a variety of late night and early morning television shows, as well as landed syncs in films, TV shows, video
games and commercials for brands including Nike, Acura, Mitsubishi, Verizon, FIFA, and Grand Theft Auto, among many others. ATC SOUND employs an expert staff that provides strategic
marketing, A&R, publicity, street team marketing, digital marketing and social media support

For all inquiries: info@atcsound.com
